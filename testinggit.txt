Support Services are available through the Zscaler Help Portal and Admin Portal. Standard Support provides Zscaler helpdesk access only during 8x5 business hours, based on your geography. Premium Support, Premium Support Plus, Premium Support Plus 16, and Premium Plus Support 24 provide Zscaler helpdesk access 24x7x365.

Upon reporting the incident (via phone, web form, or a customer's administrative UI), it will be assigned a unique Support ID number that must be used in all future correspondence until the incident is resolved. Standard Support is included in the fees for all products; Premium Support and Premium Plus Support packages can be purchased for an additional fee.
